# Code Of Conduct

SelfHarness uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and technically specific.
- Assume good intent while still naming risks clearly.
- Respect privacy around operator evidence, keys, corpora, and infrastructure.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project and its safety boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private credentials, corpora, logs, or operator artifacts.
- Misrepresenting local evidence as live benchmark reproduction.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers redirect them.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation for
behavior that harms the project or its contributors.
